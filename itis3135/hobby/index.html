When are the best times I like to read?

generate an image of a warm house in a snow storm

Any time is a good time to read! However, I find that reading after doing my homework and before bed is the most relaxing and enjoyable time. It helps me unwind and clear my mind before going to sleep. Sometimes I can find some time while waiting for doctors appointments or other errands to read a few pages.


My favorite seasonal time to read is during the fall and winter months. The cooler weather and shorter days make it the perfect time to curl up with a good book and a warm blanket. Drinking a warm beverage like hot cocoa with marshmellows while reading really makes the experience way better! It melts all my worries away

How did I start reading?

generate an image of a mother reading to her child at night

It all started really when I was a kid and my mom used to read me bedtime stories. I really loved those picturebooks of the classic fairy tales like The 500 Hats Of Bartholomew Cubbins and the Cars picture books. As I got older I started reading chapter books like Magic Tree House and Diary of a Wimpy Kid. Then of course I got into the Harry Potter series and that really sparked my love for reading.


But reading for school was always a chore for me. I kinda lost my will to read for a while in middle school and high school due to all the required reading lists, tests, quizzes, and essays. I did very much enjoy reading shakespeare and The Outsiders in school but other than that I really stopped enjoying reading much if any other books. And it wasn't until I found manga and graphic novels that I really started to enjoy reading again albeit with more pictures funnily enough

Why do I still read

generate an image of a person reading getting smarter

To start the reasons of why I still read I though to include this quote by Mark Twain.

"The man who does not read has no advantage over the man who cannot read".

Reading is a fun way to pass the time, however it is also a great way to learn new things, expand my vocabulary, and improve my focus and concentration. Reading can also help reduce stress, improve your memory, and enhance your creativity. Many Studies have shown that reading can also improve your mental health, increase cognitive function, and improve sleep quality.

Aside from all the health benefits it is also a great way to pass the time, connect with fellow reader and friends, and enhance your grasp on the world as we know it And you can never run out of books to read! There is always something new and exciting to discover within the pages of a book.
